    About Camp Nou Stadium

    Camp Nou is the home stadium of Barcelona Football Club. It is located in the Les Corts district of Barcelona and has a capacity of nearly 100,000 spectators. The stadium has been the site of many historic matches, including World Cup and Champions League finals. It also houses the FC Barcelona Museum, which is the most visited museum in Catalonia.
